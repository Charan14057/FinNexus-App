FinNexus: Terms of Service & Privacy Policy Last Updated: March 2026 1. Nature of Service FinNexus operates strictly as a Financial Decision Layer. Not a Payment Service: FinNexus is not a bank, a wallet, or a Payment Service Provider (PSP). We do not hold, transfer, or process real-world currency. Intermediate Layer: We act as an interceptor between a QR scan and your chosen UPI application (GPay, PhonePe, etc.). The "Firewall" Principle: Our software provides risk assessments and budget alerts. The final decision to enter a PIN and authorize a payment rests solely with the user. 2. Data Privacy & "On-Device" Guarantee Your financial privacy is our core architectural priority. Local Processing: All transaction analysis—including scam detection ("The Defender") and budget tracking ("The Saver")—is performed locally on your device. Zero-Cloud Storage: FinNexus does not transmit your personal transaction history, merchant details, or budget data to our servers. Your data stays on your phone. Permissions: To function, the app requires access to: Camera: Strictly for QR code scanning. SMS (Optional): Strictly for parsing automated bank transaction alerts to update your local "Saver" dashboard. Device Storage: To maintain your local encrypted database of flagged scammers and personal limits. 3. The "Defender" Security Disclaimer While our "Defender" database is cross-referenced with verified cybersecurity datasets: No Guarantee of Absolute Safety: No cybersecurity tool is 100% foolproof. While we block known threats, new "zero-day" scam accounts may exist. FinNexus is a layer of defense, not a replacement for user vigilance. Community Flagging: By using the "Flag Scam" feature, you agree to contribute anonymous UPI IDs to our community blacklist to protect other users. 4. "The Saver" & Financial Limits Assumed Data: "The Saver" uses assumed or manually entered data to help you visualize your spending habits. Impulse Alerts: Our "Red Warning" and button-removal features are interventionist tools. By accepting these terms, you authorize the app to introduce intentional friction (Speed Bumps) into your payment flow for your own financial protection. 5. Third-Party Redirection (Deep Linking) FinNexus utilizes Android Deep Linking to launch external UPI apps. We are not responsible for the performance, security, or failures of third-party applications like GPay, PhonePe, or Paytm once the handoff is complete. 6. Limitation of Liability FinNexus shall not be held liable for any financial loss, fraudulent transactions, or budget overages incurred. The app is a tool to assist in decision-making, but the User maintains 100% responsibility for every transaction authorized.
